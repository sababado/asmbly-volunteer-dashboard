Description

The brake cartridge on the main SawStop ICS (Industrial Cabinet Saw) has fired. This typically happens when the blade contacts conductive material or skin.

Your objective: Inspect the blade for damage and install the replacement cartridge. The saw is currently tagged out and cannot be used until this maintenance is complete.

warning Safety Warning

Ensure the saw is powered off at the breaker and strictly follow Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) procedures before opening the cabinet. Do not attempt if you have not completed the volunteer safety induction.

Replacement cartridges are located in the Supervisor Cabinet (Key #4). Please log the use of the consumable in the inventory sheet attached to the cabinet door.
